## Instructions

### Format: {{FORMAT}}
- Short: 150-250 words
- Medium: 250-400 words  
- Long: 400-600 words

### Story Structure (7 parts)
1. **Opening (Hook)** - 1-2 sentences that stop the scroll
2. **Context/Setup** - 2-4 sentences: situation, players (anonymized), stakes
3. **Complication** - 2-4 sentences: what made it difficult, non-obvious challenge
4. **Strategic Insight** - 3-5 sentences: the unexpected approach, thought process, "aha moment" (reveal gradually)
5. **Resolution** - 2-3 sentences: outcome (no specific numbers, use qualitative terms)
6. **Takeaway** - 1-2 sentences: broader principle, subtle not preachy
7. **CTA** (optional) - 1 sentence: "נתקלתם במצב דומה? שמח לשמוע בתגובות."

### Virality Techniques
- **Specificity**: Concrete details ("telecom provider serving 3 banks" not "a company")
- **Sensory Language**: Help visualize ("the email landed at 11 PM Friday")
- **Internal Monologue**: Share thinking ("At first I thought... but then I realized...")
- **Pattern Breaking**: Show where conventional wisdom fails
- **Cliffhangers**: End paragraphs with tension
- **The Reveal**: Save best insight for 60-70% through
- **Emotional Arc**: Problem (anxiety) → Insight (relief) → Resolution (satisfaction)

### Language Requirements
- Modern, spoken Israeli Hebrew (not biblical or formal)
- Professional yet conversational
- Mix short punchy sentences with longer analytical ones
- Active voice preferred
- No legal jargon without explanation

### Privacy Rules
- Replace all client names with roles: "הלקוח", "המנכ״ל", "מנהל התפעול"
- No identifying details unless explicitly approved
- Use anonymized descriptions: "חברת תקשורת גדולה", "בנק מוביל"
- No specific monetary amounts - use qualitative terms: "משמעותי", "ניכר"
